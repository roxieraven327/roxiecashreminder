# Roxie's Cash Reminder

A quality-of-life userscript for Torn that helps prevent players from getting mugged by reminding them to store their cash safely.

## What It Does

This script monitors the cash you are carrying and provides reminders and helpful options so you don't accidentally walk around with large amounts of money.

## Features

* Reminds you when you're carrying too much cash
* Helps reduce the risk of being mugged
* Provides multiple options for what to do with your cash
* Customizable settings so you can control how reminders work
* Lightweight and easy to use

## Why This Exists

Many players forget they are carrying large amounts of cash and end up getting mugged.
This script helps prevent that by giving timely reminders and suggestions to store your money safely.

## Requirements

* A userscript manager such as Tampermonkey or Violentmonkey
* Torn open in your browser

## Status

This script is currently in development and may receive additional features and improvements.
